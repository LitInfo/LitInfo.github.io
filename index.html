    Welcome to Lit Info!

    This website uses Hamilton's open-source data of all the fire stations. As for a brief introduction this website uses a google map to indicate all the locations as well as your current location.

    First Step:

    In the navigational bar at the top of the page, click on one of the options to show locations on the map but they will also appear in the panel on the right side of the page.

    Second Step:

    Click one of the locations in the panel on the right side of the page to show a route on the map as well the directions to get there from your current location which will be present in the direction panel below the map.

    Third Step:

    Have fun and explore all the different fire stations in Hamilton! If you need to ever look back at these instruction, just click the "Help me" button.
